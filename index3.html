Linedancing for Beginners

    Welcome to Linedancing for Beginners, here is where you'll embark on a journey to begin your linedancing era!! We will learn easy dances, listen to great music, and overall have fun!

Below are common beginner linedances

Cupid Shuffle

The steps are within the lyric, for example; "Slide to the left, Slide to the right..." So all you have to do is listen to which direction it tells you to follow. Easy peasy!!!

Wagon Wheel

This is a 2-wall beginners dance to Darius Rucker's cover of "Wagon Wheel".

Whiskey Drinkin

A country classic - perfect for an upbeat bar dance type of night!
